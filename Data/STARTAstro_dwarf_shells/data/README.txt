Data for Little things dwarf is in this folder. 
You will find VLA HI, ground based H-alpha, and IRAC 8 micron images for a couple of galaxies. 
For some, you may find GALEX Far UV and Spitzer MIPS 24 micron images as well. 

Naming convention:

GALAXYNAME_ha.fits 
GALAXYNAME_hi.fits
GALAXYNAME_irac8.fits
GALAXYNAME_mips24.fits
GALAXYNAME_fuv.fits
